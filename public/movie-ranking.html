The Best Movies According to Aaron

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howls Moving Castle

This is my favourite movie because it was one of the first memorable movies that was introduced to me from my sisters. I remember watching it on my sisters laptop when I was at a Korean bath-house around the age of 6.

Fast and Furious: Toyko Drift

This is a close second for me, the overall Fast and Furious series is okay. But the older movies really held a warm part of my childhood. Especially Tokyo Drift, this movies introduced a whole lifestyle not only in cars, but how people at that time lived in Japan. this really struck a love for being a Young Asian male.

Deadpool

Deadpool was a solid third for me, this is because Deadpool is just a really funny movie, while containing the entertaining aspect of action. Deadpool wasn't all that memerable in my childhood, but I always went back to it because it was a classic funny movie.
